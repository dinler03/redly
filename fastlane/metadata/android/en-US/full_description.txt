Redly is a fast, minimal, read-only Reddit client for Android. It uses Reddit's public RSS/JSON feeds instead of the official API, so you can browse without an account, without API limits, and without giving up your privacy.

Redly is built around a simple idea: Reddit, quietly. No noise, no notifications, no ads, no tracking — just a clean reading experience.

Features:

* Browse posts, comments, subreddits and users
* View images, GIFs, galleries and videos
* Follow your favorite communities locally
* Save images to your device
* Share posts (optionally as old.reddit links)
* Search for posts, subreddits and users
* Material Design 3 dark theme with the "Ember Dark" palette
* Open Reddit links from other apps directly inside Redly
* Fully local — no account, no telemetry
